Purpose

This invitation-only workshop brings together a select group of leaders from industry, national laboratories, government agencies, and academia. The primary goal is to publish a perspective and develop a standardized schema for publishing polymer data and reporting machine learning models for polymers across various contexts. This initiative will establish a framework for continuous improvement, defining de facto standards for polymer data reporting.
